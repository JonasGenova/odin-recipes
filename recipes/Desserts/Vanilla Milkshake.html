Vanilla Milkshake, a smooth and creamy concoction of vanilla ice cream, milk, vanilla extract topped up with whipped cream and chocolate vermicelli for irresistible looks. Learn how to make best vanilla milk shakey following this milkshake recipe and discover why it is world’s favorite.

Ingredients List
